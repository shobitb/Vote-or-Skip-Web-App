Welcome to Vote or Skip!

What is Vote or Skip?

Vote or Skip is a ranking system based on the Condorcet method. The idea is to compare objects one pair at a time until a ranking between all objects is obtained.

How to vote:

Choose between the categories shown on the left and begin voting. If you cannot decide between two items, choose skip. You can also comment on the items in the categories -- only once, though! You can also create your own categories and add images to your items. Start voting!
